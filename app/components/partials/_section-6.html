Student Accessibility Services (SAS)

Transition evening for parents of students with disabilities

This is an opportunity to meet fellow parents of university students with disabilities; learn about the services offered through the Student Accessibility Services office and how we will support your student; and hear some success stories.

Monday, August 22 from 5 to 8 p.m.

Summer Transition Program

The Summer Transition Program (STP) is for new students at UOIT who identify as a person with a disability. This program will help you to:

  • understand the real differences between high school and university.
  • learn about self-advocacy and how to use your strengths.
  • get your campus ID.
  • become familiar with the software and learning platforms you will use regularly at UOIT.
  • learn some valuable strategies to make the most of your university experience.
  • meet some key staff who will arrange your academic and learning supports.
  • become familiar with the campus and the Oshawa area.
  • make new friends and have some fun!

Tuesday, August 23 and Wednesday, August 24 from 9 a.m. to 8 p.m.
Thursday, August 25 from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
